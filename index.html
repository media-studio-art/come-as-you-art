COME AS YOU ART

Exhibition

MEDIAART 2B03 - Photo Film IARTS 3BA3 - Artist Book: Narrative / Time MEDIAART 3IO3 - Autobiographical Documentary

Come As You Art invites viewers into a shared space where personal histories, memories, and identities are expressed across media. Through archival materials and self-reflective works, the exhibition treats storytelling as a gathering. Casual, intimate, and open to interpretation.
